Intro

Welcome to the techie Mike website

The Techie Mike website serves as a record for the different IT topics I have covered and that others might find useful for their own projects.

I offer private computer tuition, consultancy services, tutorials, website design and development, web server management, SEO, website security, networking, PC builds and repairs.

About

I originally got into computers after studying micro electronics, I designed and made motherboards, I became fascinated with what you could do with a computer, and became a self-taught techie.

After I returned from travelling for 3 years in my early 30s, I decided to study a degree in computer science, I graduated in 2010 with an honours degree in computer science and engineering, I started my own training/consultancy business Saunders IT Solutions, where I provided IT training and consultancy services for companies in the South Wales area.

I now live and work as a computer science teacher in Thailand, where I teach IGCSE Computer Science.
